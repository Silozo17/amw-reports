Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a connection?
A connection is a single link between one client and one platform. For example, connecting Google Ads and Instagram for the same client uses 2 connections.
How does the add-on connection pack work?
Each add-on pack gives you 5 extra connections at £9.99/month. These connections can be used flexibly across any of your clients — assign all 5 to one client or spread them across several.
What is white-label branding?
White-label branding lets you replace AMW Reports branding with your own. Upload your logo, set your brand colours and fonts, and even use a custom domain.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade at any time and the change takes effect immediately. If you downgrade, it takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel at any time.
What platforms are supported?
What platforms are supported?
We currently support Google Ads, Meta Ads, Google Analytics, Google Search Console, YouTube, Facebook Pages, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, TikTok Ads, Pinterest, and Google Business Profile — 12 platforms in total.
Do my clients need an account?
No. Clients can access their dashboard via a unique shareable link — no login required for them.
How often is my data synced?
Creator plans sync monthly on the 4th. Freelance plans sync weekly. Agency plans sync daily.

About AMW Reports — Built by Marketers, for Marketers

AMW Reports was born from a real problem — spending too many hours every month building client reports manually. We built the tool we wished existed.

The Problem We Solved

At AMW Media, our UK-based marketing agency, we were logging into 10 different platforms every month for every client — pulling screenshots, copying data into spreadsheets, formatting slides, and sending them out manually. It was tedious, error-prone, and took hours away from the strategic work we actually enjoyed.

We looked at the existing reporting tools on the market, but they were either too expensive for smaller agencies, too complex to set up, or lacked the white-label branding we needed to present reports as our own. So we built AMW Reports.

Our Mission

Make professional reporting accessible to every marketer — whether you run a 50-person agency, freelance on your own, manage a small business, or create content. Beautiful, branded, data-driven reports without the manual grind.

We believe great reporting should not be a luxury reserved for agencies with big budgets. That is why we offer a genuinely free plan, transparent pricing, and no per-seat fees.
